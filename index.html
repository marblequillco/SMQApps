Symposium

Your Calibre library, everywhere

A dedicated Calibre companion for iOS. Wirelessly connect to your Calibre Content Server, browse metadata, search your library, download books for offline reading, and sync reading positions back to Calibre. The modern replacement for Calibre Companion.

  • Bonjour auto-discovery of Calibre servers
  • Full metadata display including custom columns
  • Bi-directional reading position sync
  • OPDS 1.2 + 2.0 catalog support
  • Offline library with download queue
  • Multiple Calibre server support
  • Batch operations by series, tag & more
  • Apple Watch companion
  • iCloud cross-device sync
