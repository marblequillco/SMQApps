Privacy Policy

Apps Support Privacy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Your privacy matters. Galley is a local-first EPUB editor. It requires no account, uses no analytics SDKs, and keeps your EPUB projects on your device unless you choose optional iCloud sync or cloud import.

Short Version

  • We do not collect any personal data on servers we operate.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or third-party trackers.
  • We do not require an account or sign-up.
  • Your EPUB files, edits, and metadata stay on your device by default.
  • Optional iCloud Drive sync uses Apple's CloudKit; your projects sync across your own devices.
  • Optional cloud import uses OAuth you initiate; tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain.

Information We Collect

None on our servers. Galley does not collect, transmit, or store personal information on any server we operate. No analytics, no advertising identifiers, no profile building.

Data Stored on Your Device

Galley stores data locally in its sandboxed container, including:

  • EPUB project files you import or create (XHTML, CSS, images, OPF, NCX/nav)
  • Edit history and undo snapshots for the current session
  • Library metadata (titles, authors, subjects, identifiers, cover)
  • Project settings and recent files list
  • Typography and editor preferences
  • Cloud import account tokens (Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, WebDAV) — stored in iOS Keychain

iCloud Drive Sync (Galley Pro, optional)

Galley Pro can sync EPUB project files across your iPhone and iPad via iCloud Drive. Your projects remain in your own iCloud account; we cannot access them. You can turn sync off in Settings.

Cloud Storage Import (optional)

If you connect Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, or WebDAV, Galley uses that provider's OAuth flow so you can browse and import books. Credentials and tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain. We do not operate those services and do not receive your cloud passwords. You can disconnect at any time in Settings.

Network Requests

Galley only makes network requests that you initiate: ISBN metadata lookup via Google Books / Open Library, and optional cloud storage or sync connections. We do not proxy or log the contents of those requests.

Crash Reporting

We do not use third-party crash reporting. If you opt in to share crash reports with Apple via the standard iOS dialog, that data goes to Apple under their privacy policy.

In-App Purchases

Purchases (including Galley Pro) are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store payment information. Entitlement is verified on-device via StoreKit.
