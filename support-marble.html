Symposium — Calibre Companion

Getting Started

  • Launch Calibre on your computer and enable Content Server (Preferences → Sharing over the net)
  • Open Symposium — it will auto-discover your Calibre server via Bonjour
  • If auto-discovery fails, tap "Add Server" and enter your computer's IP address and port (default: 8080)

Known Issues

  • Connection refused: Verify the Calibre Content Server is running and your firewall allows port 8080 (or your custom port).
  • Metadata not updating: Pull-to-refresh the library view to fetch the latest metadata from your Calibre server.
  • Sync conflicts: If reading position sync shows stale data, tap "Force Sync" in the book details screen.
