Privacy Policy

Apps Support Privacy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Your privacy matters. Quill is an EPUB editor that works entirely on your device. It requires no account, no network connection, and no tracking.

Short Version

  • We do not collect any personal data.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or third-party trackers.
  • We do not require an account or sign-up.
  • Your EPUB files stay on your device. Quill makes no network requests unless you explicitly export or share a file.
  • Optional iCloud sync uses Apple's CloudKit with end-to-end encryption.

Information We Collect

None. Quill does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on any server we operate. No analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising identifiers, no tracking. Quill makes no network requests at all — it is a fully offline editing tool.

Data Stored on Your Device

Quill stores data locally in its sandboxed container:

  • EPUB files you open for editing
  • Application preferences (editor settings, theme, font preferences)
  • Undo/redo history

No data is ever transmitted. Quill does not connect to any server.

iCloud Sync

Quill offers optional iCloud sync for your EPUB library across your iPhone and iPad. This uses Apple's CloudKit framework with end-to-end encryption and can be disabled at any time in Settings. No sync data is accessible to us.

File Export

When you export or share an edited EPUB, Quill uses the iOS Share sheet. The file is handed directly to the app or service you choose. Quill does not transmit your files to any server we control.

Crash Reporting

We do not use third-party crash reporting. If you opt in to share crash reports with Apple via the standard iOS dialog, that data goes to Apple under their privacy policy.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store payment information. Receipt data is verified locally on your device to unlock features.
